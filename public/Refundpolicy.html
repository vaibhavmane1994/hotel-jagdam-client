Refund Policy

At Vaibhav Prakash Mane, we value our customers and strive to ensure satisfaction with every purchase. This Refund Policy explains how we handle refunds and cancellations for services/products offered through our platform.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Refunds are applicable only for purchases made directly through our website/platform.
  • To be eligible for a refund, you must request it within 7 days of the original purchase.
  • Services/products that have already been utilized, partially or fully delivered, or consumed are not eligible for refunds.

Refund Process

To initiate a refund request:

  • Email us at vaibhav.pointing@gmail.com with your order details and reason for the refund.
  • Our team will review your request and confirm eligibility within 5-7 business days.
  • Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 10-15 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Digital products once downloaded or accessed.
  • Customized or personalized services.
  • Gift cards or promotional offers.

Cancellations

You may cancel your order before it is confirmed or fulfilled. Once processing or delivery has started, cancellations may not be possible.
