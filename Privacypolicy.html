Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Vaibhav Prakash Mane and its affiliates (collectively "we", "our", "us") collect, use, share, protect or otherwise process your personal data through our website (the "Platform"). By visiting or using the Platform you consent to this Privacy Policy and to the laws of India governing data protection.

Note: The Platform is intended for users located in India; data will primarily be stored and processed in India.

Collection

We collect personal data when you register, transact, or interact with the Platform. This may include:

  • Name, date of birth, address, telephone/mobile number, email ID.
  • Payment information (with consent), such as card or bank details.
  • Biometric information (only if explicitly opted in for specific features).
  • Behavior, preferences, and usage data collected automatically for analytics (aggregated).

You may choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit your ability to use some features.

Usage

We use personal data to provide and improve our services, to fulfil orders, troubleshoot issues, personalise your experience, and communicate offers or updates. Where we use your data for marketing, you will have the option to opt-out.

Sharing

We may share personal data with:

  • Affiliated group entities and corporate partners to provide services.
  • Sellers, logistics partners, payment processors and other service providers.
  • Government or law enforcement agencies when required by law or legal process.

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices—please review their privacy policies before sharing information with them.

Security Precautions

We adopt reasonable security practices to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, disclosure, loss or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Users are responsible for protecting their own login credentials.

Data Deletion & Retention

You can delete your account from your profile/settings on the Platform, or by contacting us. Deletion will remove account data, subject to retention for legal or legitimate business reasons (e.g., pending claims, fraud prevention). We retain data no longer than necessary for the purpose it was collected, except anonymised data for analytics.

Your Rights

You may access, correct, or update your personal data using the Platform features. You may also contact us to request assistance with access or correction.

Consent

By using the Platform or providing your information, you consent to our collection, use, storage and sharing of your personal data as described in this policy. You may withdraw consent by writing to the Grievance Officer (withdrawal is not retroactive).

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated as required under applicable law. Please review this page periodically.
